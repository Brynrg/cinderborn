CINDERBORN

The Conclave Trials

Beneath the blinding glare of the Lumen Spire, the Cinder caste burns in silence — soot-stained, spent, forgotten by the radiant elite.

You are an imposter forged in secret, wearing the stolen light of a dead noble to walk among the highborn. Six great houses enter the Conclave Trials. The Arbiters swear the trial is fair. The Arbiters lie.

Conquer the houses. Free the shackled. And when the judges rig the game — take their Spire.
